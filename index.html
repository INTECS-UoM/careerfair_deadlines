Tips On Facing Online Interviews

Tips On Audio Setup

💡 Better to use headsets than a built-in laptop mic.

💡 Before you go into the interview, record some audio samples from your headset mic, and check whether you can hear the recorded sample clearly.

💡 After plugin, a headset makes sure the laptop is picking audio from the headset, not from the built-in laptop mic. To check this, turn on recording and tap the headset mic quietly and check whether that tapping sound is recorded. If not, change the audio settings to use a headset mic over the built-in laptop mic.

💡 When speaking, do not speak directly into the mic. Instead, let the mic be at the natural place after wearing the headset. Speaking directly to the mic will lead “p”,”t” and “s” sounds difficult to understand and make your speech noisy.

💡 If you’re going to use zoom for the interview, watch this to tweak your zoom app for better audio quality. Link 1 | Link 2
